Why keep an eye on the water?

Rivers and seas should be playgrounds, not health gambles. But companies are releasing untreated waste straight into waterways at an unprecedented rate. Combined with agricultural runoff and micro-plastics, it is harming wildlife and human health alike. Dirty Water is here to help you navigate these waters safely.

Our map layers live discharge alerts so you can decide whether today’`s a splash-about day or a stay-on-shore day.

Important: This Map Won’t Warn You About Water Safety Risks

It’s important to note that this map doesn’t indicate whether the water is safe. This map, the Sewage Map, is not an alert service. It shows the operational status of sewage overflows across the country, but it doesn’t provide enough context to help you assess whether it’s safe to enter the water. While it tells you where sewage overflows are, it doesn’t show exactly how the discharge may affect water quality or pose a risk to human health.
