Why keep an eye on the water?

Rivers and seas should be playgrounds, not health gambles. But companies are releasing untreated waste straight into waterways at an unprecedented rate. Combined with agricultural runoff and micro-plastics, it is harming wildlife and human health alike. Dirty Water is here to help you navigate these waters safely.
